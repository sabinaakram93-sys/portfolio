About

I am an early-career Human-Centered AI researcher working at the intersection of HCI, UX research, and responsible AI. My research examines how intelligent systems influence human interaction, decision-making, and technology adoption across recruitment, healthcare, and educational domains.

Using mixed-methods and interaction design approaches, I investigate transparency, usability, fairness, and human-AI collaboration in AI-mediated environments. I am particularly interested in conversational systems, explainable AI, and the design of socially responsible technologies that align technical innovation with human needs.

Through collaborations with academic and industry partners across Europe, I have contributed to projects on AI recruitment systems, healthcare decision-support technologies, and AI-enhanced learning environments, with research published in international HCI and Human-Centered AI venues.
